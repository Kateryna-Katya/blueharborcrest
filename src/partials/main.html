About the Game

Blueharbor Crest: Signal Watch places players in charge of a remote coastal outpost responsible for monitoring maritime signals.
Messages arrive as sequences of light patterns, tones, or distant visual cues that must be interpreted correctly.
We don't hold your hand with tutorials - you'll just start to get it as you play.
Over time, signals become more complex, requiring stronger focus and memory.

Core Gameplay

  • Observe light patterns from distant sources

  • Interpret signals based on learned patterns

  • Respond with correct signal outputs

  • Track multiple sources simultaneously

  • Maintain accuracy over extended sessions

Mistakes don’t end the game, but shift how future signals appear.
